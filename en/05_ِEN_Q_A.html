Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • Where is TALABY Foundation headquarter?

    - Riyadh City

  • How do I link my store to my order?

    * By following these steps:
    1- Register on TALABY website or application
    2- Contact us through the following email: info@talaby.sa

  • Is there a cash on delivery payment?

    - Yes

  • I have a store on Instagram, can I register on TALABY?

    - Yes, but on condition that a commercial register or freelance document is available

  • How long does it take to deliver within Riyadh?

    - On the same day

  • How do I track orders as a customer?

    - Through the store's control panel on TALABY Application

  • How can I track the status of my order?

    - Through the tracking number sent to you on TALABY website

  • Is there a refrigerated transport?

    - Currently there is no refrigerated transport

  • Is there a fee for rejected or recovered parcels?

    - No, only the delivered parcels are paid

  • Is TALABY accepting liquid products such as chemical products?

    - No liquid products are not accepted in TALABY

  • How many delivery attempts are available for each parcel?

    - Three attempts for each parcel and then we return it to the store

  • Can I change the address after the policy is issued?

    - Yes, by contacting technical support on the following number:
    0534401474

  • What are the dimensions of the package?

    - 35×35×35 cm

  • What is the maximum weight of the parcel?

    - The maximum weight is 25 kg

  • What is the situation in case of parcel loss or damage?

    - The policy number will be sent to the following: info@talaby.sa
    And we promise to solve the problem as soon as possible
